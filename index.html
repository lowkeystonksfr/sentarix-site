The real problem

Digital systems shift constantly. One moment feels responsive and the next feels delayed. Users notice these changes immediately because the feel never stays stable.

Frames feel different

Even with high frame rates, small variations disrupt consistency. Inputs swing between heavy and light, so control never fully locks into place.

Inputs never align

The same action can feel crisp in one moment and sluggish in another. No system today provides a stable feel for every input.

Instability is constant

Hardware differences, load, and background processes shift perception in subtle but meaningful ways. These shifts build up and break the experience.

What SentariX provides

SentariX gives your experience something it has never had before. A steady, unified feel that holds from moment to moment for every user.

A stable step for your loop

SentariX outputs a refined, consistent step value that removes jitter from perception entirely. Your update loop becomes clean and stable.

Equal feel across hardware

Users on different devices share the same mechanics and timing feel. No hidden advantages or disadvantages remain.

A sealed drop-in engine

You provide minimal readings and receive a fully refined step back. The internal process remains sealed and self-regulated.

Training and simulation
