The real problem

Every digital system has small moment-to-moment shifts that change how the experience feels. Some moments feel responsive, others feel delayed. Users notice it instantly — even when they can’t explain it.

Frames never feel identical

Even at high frame rates, subtle variations break consistency. Some inputs feel heavy, others too light — and control never truly locks in.

Inputs land differently

The same action can feel crisp one moment and sluggish the next. No system today guarantees that every input is handled with the same feel.

Hidden instability everywhere

Hardware differences, load, and environmental noise all change user perception in subtle ways — stacking up into a noticeable inconsistency.

What SentariX provides

SentariX gives your experience one thing it has never had before: consistent feel for every user, regardless of conditions.

A stable step for your loop

SentariX outputs a clean, consistent step value your engine applies each update, removing jitter from perception entirely.

Equal feel across hardware

Users on different devices finally share the same mechanics and timing feel — leveling the field and removing hidden advantages.

Drop-in sealed engine

The engine is fully sealed. You pass in a few readings and get a refined step back — with zero exposure of internal logic.

Training and simulation
