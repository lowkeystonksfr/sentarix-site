Working with SentariX

Engagement path

SentariX is licensed as an engine, not a commodity library. The goal is to prove value quickly, then scale in a way that matches the reach of your experience.

Stages

Focused pilot

A time boxed integration with a small surface. The aim is to feel the difference with your own content and players, and to gather both subjective and objective signals of benefit.

Expansion inside a title

Once the effect is clear, SentariX can be expanded inside that title or experience. This can include more modes, more regions, or more types of interaction.

Portfolio and platform level

At scale, SentariX can become a shared engine layer across multiple experiences, with central support and domain specific tuning where needed.

License outline

Specific terms depend on the size of the opportunity, the level of support, and the degree of exclusivity. The common pattern is a yearly engine license for one or more titles, with an optional success component as the value proves out.
