Why timing consistency matters

Without steady timing, a system slowly becomes harder to use. Movements lose accuracy, actions feel delayed, and two users no longer share the same experience. This leads to loss of trust, loss of clarity, and widening gaps between players or participants.

Moment-to-moment drift

Unclear response

Inputs feel different from earlier moments, making precision difficult.

Unequal environments

Each user interacts under different conditions, making shared experiences harder to deliver fairly.
