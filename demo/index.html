Feel test

Experience SentariX

The clearest way to understand SentariX is to feel the difference between raw conditions and the stable perceived timeline it provides.

Core comparison idea

Every demo follows the same pattern. One side shows the raw world with its natural jitter and variation. The other side runs on SentariX and uses the stable step value. Even with identical reported values, the feel becomes very different.

Visual feel demo

A simple scene shows two systems side by side. Both receive the same input and nominal conditions. The raw side looks restless and uneven. The SentariX side moves with a smooth, precise rhythm that feels immediately different.

Click and reaction tests

Local click timing can be fed into SentariX and traced against raw measurements. Raw intervals jump around. SentariX intervals stay fixed at the same value, giving a very clear sense of stability for rapid input sequences.

Frame chart visualisation

Charts with raw samples on top and SentariX step values below make the effect visible at a glance. A noisy line above and a flat line below show exactly what the user feels in motion.

Next steps
